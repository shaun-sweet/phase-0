1.1 Think About Time Reflection

Most of the time management and productivity ideas I knew about prior. One that was new was the “Pomodoro” technique. Time boxing is a strategy that is incorporated in the Pomodoro technique. It is basically the idea that you will set aside a certain amount of time to do only one, or a group of things, and only those things. I can use that to make seemingly endless work broken into bite sized chunks and see measurable, consistent progress.
